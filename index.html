How to play: Search for any animal to compare it to today's target. Taxonomy chips trace the tree of life — green = match, red = where it diverges. The chain stops at the first mismatch. Use Guess the Diet and Guess the Range to unlock Field Notes early. Hover any guess card for its Wikipedia image and description.

Taxonomist Mode lets you guess individual taxonomic levels (family, order, etc.) to reveal those levels in the tree without spending an animal guess.

Proximity % reflects relatedness weighted by taxonomic depth and group size.
